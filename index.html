About UPL League

UPL League is a competitive Roblox football league focused on realism, organization, and player development. Our goal is to create a structured environment where players can compete and improve.

Who Are We

We are a community-driven football organization built by passionate players. UPL ensures fair play, competitive balance, and a professional league experience.

How are stats tracked?
Stats are manually updated after matches.
What do classes mean?
